TrapX Declenchement Rev 2.0

2-layer LoRa remote trap trigger for a wildlife monitoring system. A 12V field supply steps down through a buck converter to 5V then an LDO to 3.3V, powering an STM32 microcontroller and an EBYTE sub-GHz LoRa module for long-range wireless triggering. SMA antenna, dedicated output terminal, plus SWD and UART headers for field debugging without disassembly.
