The core craft

PCB Design & Layout

It starts with the board. Multi-layer schematic capture and layout in KiCad, Altium, and Eagle, from 2-layer IoT boards to dense mixed-signal and RF designs. Clean copper, verified against the fab from the first net.

Embedded Firmware

Bring-up and firmware for ESP32, STM32, and nRF52: drivers, comms stacks, and hardware-software integration.

Product & Enclosure Design

3D CAD enclosures and photorealistic KeyShot renders, from sheet-metal housings to consumer hero shots.

Turnkey Manufacturing

Design Audits & DFM

LoRa · Wildlife Monitoring

TrapX Declenchement Rev 2.0

2-layer LoRa remote trap trigger for a wildlife monitoring system. A 12V field supply steps down through a buck converter to 5V then an LDO to 3.3V, powering an STM32 microcontroller and an EBYTE sub-GHz LoRa module for long-range wireless triggering. SMA antenna, dedicated output terminal, plus SWD and UART headers for field debugging without disassembly.
